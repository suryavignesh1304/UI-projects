About Our Bistro

Bringing Flavors Since 1998

Junior's Grill & Bistro has been a staple in the culinary scene since 1998. Founded by Chef Martin in the heart of downtown, we are committed to bringing flavors from around the world to your plate.
Our philosophy revolves around using only the freshest, seasonal ingredients to create dishes that delight the senses.

We pride ourselves on offering an exceptional dining experience, from our warm and inviting ambiance to our attentive service. Whether you're joining us for a casual lunch or a special dinner, expect nothing but the best at Savor & Spice Bistro.
